About Us

Welcome to Chicken Pastil House, the home of authentic, flavor-packed pastil in P1 Bololo, Guinobatan. Our recipe is rooted in tradition, using only the freshest chicken and spices sourced from local markets to bring the comforting taste of home to every bite.

We believe in quality over quantity, preparing fresh batches daily to ensure every pastil meets our high standards. From our family to yours, we're proud to serve Albay with love and dedication.
